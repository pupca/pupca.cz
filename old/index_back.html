Hello there! My name’s Steven and I’m a web designer from London, UK. I believe in clean and minimalistic design and also think it is very important to make a web site compatible with all modern devices, like phones and tablets.

Feel free to check out my work or view my resume. I am currently available for hire, so if I sound fit for your next project, don’t hesitate to contact me!
